The obxium readymade token introduces an exciting new concept to the crypto & digital art space by representing the entirety of an artist through a single Ethereum non-fungible token (NFT), which you can purchase today. Ownership of the obxium readymade token grants the token owner the ultimate form of artwork & unlimited freedom of expression.

As the token owner you can choose to become obxium or save this piece of art history forever!

About obxium

obxium is the identity of an established digital artist who has been creating at the forefront of the crypto art movement since the beginning of 2018. The artist has primarily used two dimensional illustration comprised of warped emoji and other pictographic elements along with influences and palettes from vaporwave and synthwave culture. Expression in most art communicates messaging about simulation theory, consumerism and contemporary culture along with specific statements about cryptocurrency and crypto art.

In just under 3 years, the artist has created approximately 175 digital artworks including collaborations with leading crypto artists such as Alina Loseva, Reinhard Schmid, OficinasTK, Coldie and others. obxium has sold work through all major crypto art platforms, given works & their proceeds to charities, created resources such as the awesome crypto art list for the community, and befriended numerous artists and collectors along the way.

Later, the artist began to write a book for crypto artists (the Crypto Art Crash Course, whose manuscript is part of the obxium readymade token) and a newsletter about the crypto art scene raising tough questions and pondering the oddness in this ecosystem through some challenging articles along the way.

Detailed History

obxium has been creating and selling crypto art just after the infamous cryptocurrency all time highs and right at the beginning of the Rare Pepe downfall. The artist has only ever created on the Bitcoin and Ethereum blockchains.

In fact, obxium has only one Rare Pepe, PEPEGIANT to their credit as a certified rare Pepe despite creating some 15 works to submit to the scientists. Unfortunately they hung up their lab coats just before obxium could submit more Pepes and begin to realize Rare Pepe fame.

The Bitcoin and Counterparty (XCP) and Rare Pepe ecosystem never quite took off. There are many reasons why this is so, from changes in the BTC specification which had negative implications on XCP, to infighting and good old poor adoption.

However, hot on the heels of this was Ethereum and smart contract powered crypto art projects and platforms like CryptoPunks, Curio Cards, KnownOrigin, SuperRare, MakersPlace and many others.

obxium has always supported diversity in artistic exposure, so diving into new communities and currencies was a welcome endeavor. The majority of creation has occurred on Ethereum in the form of NFT based artworks since 2018.
