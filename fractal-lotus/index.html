FRACTAL LOTUS

Fractal Lotus is a limited single-edition fine cryptoart series by obxium

Presenting eight ephemeral objects which can be attached to your preferred reality as desired.

UPDATE: 5 of the 8 FRACTAL LOTUS pieces have sold.

Only 3 remain available for acquisition.

  • FRACTAL LOTUS 4
  • FRACTAL LOTUS 5
  • FRACTAL LOTUS 6

Contact for more information

FRACTAL LOTUS represents the blooming energy and culture of today’s advanced netizens, the degens of a new decentralized web 3 world. Each object has unique character and personality with specific care taken to choose only the most based of textural flavors.

The series is offered on the Kusama network blockchain as tokens minted under the RMRK specification with the collection being defined in Kusama block 5419694.

The included 3D objects can be used as demonstrated, in web or mobile applications or you can load the Adobe Aero objects into the Aero app and apply the objects in your particular flavor of reality.

FRACTAL LOTUS represents the genesis project in a new range of interactive art that invites the collector to collaborate with the artist by using the art objects in new and exciting ways. What can you come up with? Will you share it with the world or keep the enjoyment close? The possibilities are limited only to your imagination!

Each piece is a single edition experience that includes enhanced media and licensing rights.

The series is limited to only 8 released pieces and 2 artist’s proofs.

Click or tap each FRACTAL LOTUS to visit the page about it.
