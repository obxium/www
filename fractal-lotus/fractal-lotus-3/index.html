FRACTAL LOTUS 3 is a complete cryptoart experience featuring the beauty of a lotus flower combined with the infinite worlds of artificial intelligence.

About FRACTAL LOTUS 3

The artwork owner receives a Kusama RMRK token, with links to the original PNG art on IPFS.
