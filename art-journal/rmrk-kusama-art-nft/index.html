RMRK Kusama Art NFTs

2020.11.12 – I have created and released a limited edition art series as Kusama blockchain non-fungible tokens (NFTs) using the RMRK NFT specification. This entry shows off the work and describes in detail, the process used for minting the collection and individual NFTs.

I am excited to announce the release of 5 limited edition art NFTs minted according to the RMRK NFT specification version 0.1 on the Kusama blockchain!

These 3D rendered works celebrate age old platonic solids, vaporwave aesthetics, and the Kusama blockchain itself. The first work, “Kusama Cube” was also transferred as a gesture of goodwill to Bruno Skvorc, creator of the RMRK NFT solution.

All of the source files defining this historical art project can be found in https://github.com/obxium/oksm.
